Open Tax Solver 2020

OTS is a safe, secure, easy-to-use program for calculating Tax Return Form entries

Determine your tax-owed or refund-due for Federal or State personal income taxes. Includes updated versions for US 1040, Schedules A, B, C, D and tax forms for individual states. OTS provides both Graphical and Textual User Interfaces for quickly filling out your tax forms.

Features

  • Graphical and Textual User Interfaces
  • Straightforward - complete your tax returns quickly
  • Safe / Secure - Runs directly on your PC - no web connections or cloaked software.
  • Directly fills-out Tax Forms for printing
